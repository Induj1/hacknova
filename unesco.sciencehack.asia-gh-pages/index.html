Frequently Asked Questions

What is the theme of the Hackathon?

The theme for the Hackathon is Cybersecurity. Participants are encouraged to develop innovative solutions and tools to tackle real-world cybersecurity challenges.

What is the date and duration of the Hackathon?

The Hackathon will be held on 16th & 17th December, lasting for 48 hours. Get ready for an exciting, fast-paced competition!

Who can participate in the Hackathon?

The Hackathon is open to all MAHE Bengaluru students. So, gather your team and prepare to showcase your cybersecurity skills!

How can I register for the Hackathon?

Registration is open for team leaders only. To register, please fill out the form here: Register Now.

Is this Hackathon team-based?

Yes, this Hackathon is team-based. You can form a team and collaborate to solve cybersecurity challenges. Team sizes will be specified in the guidelines.

What is the prize pool for the Hackathon?

Can we submit pre-existing code for the competition?

For this Hackathon, all code should be new and developed during the competition. Existing apps or codebases are not eligible unless significantly modified to meet the theme of the event.

Where will the Hackathon take place?

The Hackathon will be offline at the MIT Bengaluru campus. Be prepared to immerse yourself in a productive and challenging environment.

What is the registration deadline?

Register your team before the event on 16th December. Make sure your team leader completes the registration form on time!

What tools or resources should we have for the Hackathon?

You should have your development environment set up and any tools or software needed for cybersecurity challenges. We will provide specific details in the pre-event instructions.

The hackathon will take place at MIT Bengaluru on 16th and 17th December 2024. Participants will work in teams to brainstorm, design, and build solutions addressing real-world cybersecurity problems.
